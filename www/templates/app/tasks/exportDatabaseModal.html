Export Database

It looks like you're using this feature for the first time, here's something you should be aware of.

Corvus can export a database to your Google Drive, to do so it will prompt you to log into your Google account and give your permissions to access Drive.

Then it will create a Corvus folder where it will upload your database exports.

Corvus will only ever access your Drive for this purpose and nothing else. Also, it won't touch anything outside the Corvus folder.

Please note that the file you export from your RavenDB server will have to be downloaded to your device first, which may not be possible for large databases.
